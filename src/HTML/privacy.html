Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Courserr. We are a student-built course discovery platform for Ames High School, and we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Courserr is an open-source project created by students, for students. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics data about how you use Courserr, such as which pages you visit and which features you use most.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to remember your preferences and improve your experience.
  • Local Storage: Data stored locally in your browser, including your cookie consent preferences.

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or school ID.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Improve and optimize Courserr based on usage patterns
  • Understand which features are most helpful to students
  • Fix bugs and improve overall performance
  • Remember your privacy preferences

Cookies & Analytics

When you first visit Courserr, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to:

  • Accept: Allow anonymous analytics to help us improve the site
  • Decline: Opt out of all analytics tracking

If you decline, we do not collect any analytics data. Your choice is stored locally and will be remembered on future visits.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with:

  • Future developers working on Courserr (for improvement purposes)
  • No third-party advertising networks
  • No commercial data brokers

Third-Party Services

Courserr may contain links to external websites or services, including:

  • Ames High School course handbook
  • Clubs and activities information
  • GitHub (for our open-source project)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Courserr is designed for high school students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we may have collected (which, as noted above, is minimal)
  • Delete your local storage data (clear your browser cache)
  • Opt out of analytics at any time by declining cookies

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
